ABOUT US

The Charcoal Experts

Established in 2012, IDN Coal has grown to be an industry leading and benchmark setting charcoal manufacturer. Our factories produce in excess of 300 tons of premium coconut charcoal briquettes per month for reputed shisha and barbeque charcoal brands and importers from the Middle East, Europe, and the Americas.

We only use the highest quality of coconut shell charcoal, most of which we make ourselves, to manufacture our briquettes. Our ability to mass produce raw coconut charcoal gives us impressive cost savings and quality control, allowing us to offer the best prices to our customers, as well as consistent product delivery. Our vast infrastructure and logistics network allow us to deliver with the shortest possible lead time to any destination around the world. Our briquettes are resistant to cracking, have low ash content and give a high heat and long burn time. Due to the low volatile matter content, they do not give off any smoke or unpleasant odours which may affect the taste of shisha tobacco. We produce briquettes in a variety of shapes and sizes and can package them in our customers' desired box and brand design requirements. We are your one stop charcoal solutions provider!   

Delivering the Quality you expect

We use only 100% coconut shell charcoal with high fixed carbon content and low ash,for our shisha  briquettes and do not add any fillers such as wood charcoal and sawdust or any chemicals such as lime and nitrates. We control every step of the briquette manufacturing process from raw charcoal production in our carbonization kilns to final briquette packaging and delivery. Our fully equipped in house laboratory analyses every batch of briquettes produced to ensure product consistency. Through years of intensive research and development we have perfected our briquette formulas and production processes. Numerous factors affect the quality of charcoal briquettes including raw material selection and cleaning, mixing consistency, blending and moulding temperature and drying duration and methods. Our quality control teams meticulously monitor each of these steps to ensure every batch of briquettes produced is exactly what our customers have ordered, shipment after shipment.
